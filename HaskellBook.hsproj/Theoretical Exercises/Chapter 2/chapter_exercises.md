# Parenthization

1.  
  ```haskell
  2 + 2 * 3 - 1
  2 + (2 * 3) - 1
  ```

2. 
  ```haskell
  (^) 10 $ 1 + 1
  10 ^ (1 + 1)
  ```
  
3. 
  ```haskell
  2 ^ 2 * 4 ^ 5 + 1
  (2 ^ 2) * (4 ^ 5) + 1
  ```
